Grandpa Gs Meat Balls

Ingredients

------------------------

    Veal/Ground Beef Mix
    Italian bread crumbs
    2 Eggs
    Salt
    Peper
    Orgeno

Making the Meat Balls

Grab a large mixing bowl and add 1 pound of a beef and veal mixture. Season with salt and pepper, then crack in two eggs. Using your hands, mix everything together until well combined. Gradually add Italian breadcrumbs, one cup at a time, until the mixture is moist but firm. This ensures the meatballs hold together in the sauce without falling apart. If you need a sauce recipe, check out Grandpa G's Tomato Sauce. Once mixed, shape the meat into balls and place them on a tray or plate that can fit in the fridge. Let them chill for an hour to set.

Cooking the Meat Balls

Boiled(in sauce)

Take you chilled meat balls drop them into the sauce, let them cook in the sauce for 1hr to 1 1/2 hr. Then there done!

Baked(in sauce)

Pre-Heat Oven to 400F, get a deep bakeing dish the drizil it with olive oil. Take your sauce add it to the dish the lay the meat balls on top. add more sauce then cover with tin foil. Insert the pan into the oven for 30 minuites. Once pulled out they are ready to eat!!
