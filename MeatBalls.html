Grandpa Gs Meat Balls

Ingredients

------------------------

    Veal/Ground Beef Mix
    Italian bread crumbs
    2 Eggs
    Salt
    Peper
    Orgeno

Making the Meat Balls

Grab your self a large mixing bowl and put 1lb of Beef Veil Mix. Add salt and peper the the two eggs. Mix with your hands. After it is mixed add 1 cup at a time of the itlaian bread crums.You want the meat balls to remain moist and firm. If you dont they will fall apart in the sauce. If you need a sauce recipe you can find Grandpa G's Tomato Sauce Then take the raw meat and make them into balls, put them on somthing you can stick in the fridge so they caan set. Let them set for an hour.

Cooking the Meat Balls

Boiled(in sauce)

Take you chilled meat balls drop them into the sauce, let them cook in the sauce for 1hr to 1 1/2 hr. Then there done!

Baked(in sauce)

Pre-Heat Oven to 400F, get a deep bakeing dish the drizil it with olive oil. Take your sauce add it to the dish the lay the meat balls on top. add more sauce the cover with tin foil. Insert the pan into the oven for 30 minuites. Once pulled out they are ready to eat!!
