Granpa G's Lasagna

You're almost ready for the perfect Sunday dinner. You've got the sauce, the meatballs, the steak, and the sausage all that's missing is the pasta.

Ingredients

------------------------

    1lb of Lesagna pasta
    Mozzarella
    Ricotta
    Crumpled Meatballs
    Tomato Sauce

Id Suggest using Grandpa Gs Meat Balls and Grandpa G's Tomato Sauce but you can use store bought as well, but i cant say it will be as good.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------

Making your Lesagna

To make a classic lasagna, start by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C) and lightly greasing a deep baking dish with olive oil. Cook lasagna noodles in a large pot of boiling water until al dente, then drain and set them aside. Begin assembling the lasagna by placing a layer of noodles on the bottom of the baking dish, followed by a thin, even layer of tomato sauce and a sprinkle of shredded cheese. Repeat these layers until the dish is almost full. For the final layer, top with more sauce, a generous amount of cheese, and, if desired, a sprinkle of fresh basil or red pepper flakes. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 20 minutes. Let it cool slightly before serving. Enjoy your homemade lasagna!
